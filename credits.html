This visualization is an attempt to cull some salient information about the definition of what it means to be a documentary film from the many books on the topic.

The text from books on the topic of defining documentaries that we used can be found at http://johngriersonhasalottoanswerfor.blogspot.com/

Made by four students at MIT in William Uricchio's "Innovation in Documentary" course:

  • Jessica Kenney
  • Ciara Kamahele
  • Miles Steele
  • Brandon Vasquez
